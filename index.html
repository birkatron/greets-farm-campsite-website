About Greets Farm

Not many places.
The right ones.

Tucked away on a small family farm in the Howardian Hills Area of Outstanding Natural Beauty, Greets Farm is a level, two-acre certificated site offering tranquillity, space and spectacular countryside views — with the added bonus of being just a mile's easy stroll from the charming village of Welburn.

Our adults-only site has been welcoming guests for over 15 years. Visitors love the rural farm feel — sheep grazing in neighbouring fields, views stretching toward Castle Howard — yet without feeling cut off from good food, local attractions and excellent transport links.

Sarah and Marcus Lyon run the site with the care and attention that only a small, family-run place can offer. Five pitches. Open countryside. Castle Howard just down the road.

Why choose us

Why Greets Farm?

The field is flat. The village has a good pub. The bus stops nearby. A lot of our guests come back. Make of that what you will.

Howardian Hills AONB

Outstanding natural beauty on the doorstep. Walk from the gate into some of North Yorkshire's finest countryside.

Level Field, Easy Access

A wide stoned track, a level two-acre field. No tight corners, no awkward reversing. Most sizes of motorhome and caravan are welcome.

Village on the Doorstep

The Crown & Cushion pub and Dogh artisan bakery are a pleasant mile's stroll away in Welburn. No on-site shop needed.

Yorkshire Coastliner Bus Stop

Five minutes' walk from the site. York or the Yorkshire Coast without driving, parking or traffic. Leave the motorhome exactly where it is.

Castle Howard — Walk, Cycle or Drive

One of England's great country houses, 3 miles away. Walk there on foot in under an hour — it's a beautiful 2.7-mile route through the Howardian Hills. View the OS Maps walking route →

Solo Travellers Welcome

A £5 reduction in nightly site fees for solo campers. A small, friendly site — never isolated. Many of our regulars travel solo.
