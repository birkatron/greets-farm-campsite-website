Finding us

Directions to Greets Farm

Greets Farm is located near the village of Welburn in the Howardian Hills, North Yorkshire. The postcode for your sat nav is YO60 7EP. Access to the site is via a wide stoned track — easy for caravans and motorhomes of all sizes, with no tight corners or awkward reversing required.

Sat nav note: Use postcode YO60 7EP. The site entrance is on a quiet country lane — follow the stoned track to the field. If you have any difficulty finding us, call 07918 070716.

Driving Directions

Greets Farm is easily reached from both north and south via the A64 York–Scarborough road.

From the South (York & A64 westbound)

1

From York, take the A64 eastbound towards Scarborough.

2

Continue on the A64 past Barton Hill. After approximately 12 miles from York, look for the Welburn / Castle Howard turn-off.

3

Turn left onto the B1257 towards Welburn and Castle Howard.

4

Continue through the village of Welburn. Greets Farm is on the outskirts of the village — follow the lane and look for the entrance to the stoned track on your right.

5

Follow the stoned track to the campsite field. Easy access — no tight turns required.

From the North (Scarborough & A64 eastbound)

1

From Scarborough or Malton, take the A64 westbound towards York.

2

Pass through Malton (5 miles from the site). Continue west on the A64.

3

Look for the Welburn / Castle Howard turn-off on the right, onto the B1257.

4

Continue through the village of Welburn. Greets Farm is on the outskirts — follow the lane and look for the entrance to the stoned track.

5

Follow the stoned track to the campsite field. Easy access — no tight turns required.

Towing a caravan or driving a large motorhome? The access track is wide and well-maintained. Most sizes of outfit are welcome. If you have a particularly large vehicle, drop us an email first and we'll confirm suitability.

Without the car

Public Transport

Once you're pitched up, you don't need the car for a day out. The Yorkshire Coastliner bus stop is five minutes' walk from the site.

Yorkshire Coastliner Bus

Five minutes' walk from the site. Direct services to York and the Yorkshire Coast (Scarborough, Filey, Whitby). Leave the motorhome exactly where it is.

Malton Railway Station

Five miles away. Malton is on the York–Scarborough line, with regular services. A taxi or bus from Malton to the site takes around 15 minutes.

Walking to Castle Howard

Castle Howard is a beautiful 2.7-mile walk from the site through the Howardian Hills — one of the finest short walks in North Yorkshire. Allow around 50–60 minutes each way. View the full OS Maps route →

Cycling

Castle Howard is a pleasant 3-mile cycle from the site through the Howardian Hills. The lanes are quiet and the scenery is outstanding. Bikes are welcome on site.
