Local area

What's Nearby

The Howardian Hills put a remarkable amount within easy reach — from a village pub a mile away to York in under an hour by bus.

Village & Local

The Crown & Cushion, Welburn 18th century village pub, dog-welcoming and very good. A mile's easy stroll from the site.
Dogh — Artisan Bakery & Café Morning coffee and warm pastries in the village. The ideal start to a day in the Howardian Hills.
Yorkshire Coastliner Bus Stop Five minutes' walk from the site. Direct services to York and the Yorkshire Coast — no car needed.

Wider Area — Day Trips

Castle Howard — 3 miles by road  ·  2.7 miles on foot Magnificent estate, world-class gardens, café and house. Drive, cycle, or walk the 2.7-mile OS Maps route through the Howardian Hills.
Malton — 5 miles Yorkshire's food capital. Independent shops, food markets, excellent restaurants and Eden Camp museum.
York — 13 miles Historic city, city walls, the Shambles, excellent restaurants. Easily reached by Coastliner bus.
Yorkshire Lavender Farm & Terrington Nearby Howardian Hills village and lavender farm — beautiful in summer.
North York Moors & Dalby Forest The North York Moors, North Yorkshire Moors Railway and Dalby Forest are all within easy reach.
Yorkshire Coast — Scarborough, Filey, Whitby Easy day trips to the coast. Whitby Abbey, Scarborough Castle, Filey Brigg — all within an hour.
Howardian Hills Footpaths & Bridleways Walking and cycling routes from the gate. The Howardian Hills at their finest, on your doorstep.
