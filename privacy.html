Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Who We Are

This website is operated by Greets Farm Campsite, Welburn, North Yorkshire, YO60 7EP. You can contact us at info@greetsfarm.co.uk or by calling 07918 070716.

What Data We Collect

When you use the contact or booking enquiry form on this website, we collect the information you provide: your name, email address, phone number, and any details you include in your message. This information is used solely to respond to your enquiry and to manage your booking.

We also use Google Analytics to understand how visitors find and use this website. Google Analytics collects anonymised data about your visit, including pages viewed, time spent on the site and how you arrived. This data does not identify you personally.

How We Use Your Data

We use the information you provide through the contact form to respond to your enquiry and, if you make a booking, to manage that booking. We do not use your data for marketing purposes and we do not share it with third parties, except where required by law.

Google Analytics data is used to improve this website and to understand which pages and content are most useful to visitors.

Cookies

This website uses cookies for Google Analytics. These are analytics cookies that help us understand how visitors use the site. They do not store personal information. You can accept or decline these cookies via the banner displayed when you first visit the site.

You can also control cookies through your browser settings. For more information about cookies and how to manage them, visit allaboutcookies.org.

Data Retention

We retain booking and enquiry information for as long as is necessary to fulfil your booking and for a reasonable period afterwards for administrative purposes. If you would like us to delete your data, please contact us at info@greetsfarm.co.uk.

Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to access the personal data we hold about you, to request its correction or deletion, and to object to its processing. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at info@greetsfarm.co.uk.

If you have a concern about how we handle your data, you have the right to complain to the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

External Links

This website contains links to external websites (including Castle Howard, the Yorkshire Coastliner, and others). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those websites and recommend you review their privacy policies separately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page shows when it was last updated.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at info@greetsfarm.co.uk or call 07918 070716.
